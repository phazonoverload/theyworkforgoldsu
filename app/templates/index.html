Hold your student representatives accountable.

Voted in to fight for you. What is done in your name?

Goldsmiths Students' Union is spearheaded by elected representatives who represent and support them in all aspects of life at the college. Elected each year, they are voted in based on manifesto promises which they hope to achieve by the end of their term.

They Work For GoldSU is an independent platform makes the actions of elected officials at Goldsmiths Students' Union more accessible and transparent. Understand their promises and progress by exploring the site.
